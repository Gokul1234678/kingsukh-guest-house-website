About Us

The Best Holidays Begin Here!

                Welcome to King Sukh Guest House, your gateway to
                serenity nestled amidst the breathtaking landscapes of
                West Bengal. Tucked away near the scenic wonders of
                Biharinath Hill, Baranti Hill, Susunia Hill, Joychandi Hill,
                Garhpanchkot, Baranti Dam, Maithon Dam, and the
                enchanting Panchat Dam, our guest house offers the
                perfect escape into nature’s embrace.

                Immerse yourself in the tranquility of our lush garden oasis,
                where comfort meets elegance. Whether you're here to
                relax or explore, we provide the perfect setting for both.
                Savor delightful meals crafted with care, unwind in our
                serene surroundings, and discover the hidden gems of
                Purulia. At King Sukh Guest House, every moment is a
                memory in the making, with heartfelt hospitality that
                ensures your stay is as warm as it is memorable.
